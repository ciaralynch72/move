Move Mo Chara Blog was created to help be a hub for my passion project, Move Mo Chara Podcast.

Move Mo Chara came about because I am passionate about movement in all forms and I have a natuaral curiosity about what drives people who's careers are built on movement. The podcast is more focused on the movement of body, however, I love movement in all shapes and forms. From moving abroad, careers and travel and most of all, challenging myself to move out of my comfort zone.

I wanted to create a hub where I can expand Mo Move Chara beyond the podcast and explore movement in all avenues of life.

Albert Einstein said it the best - Nothing happens until something moves!
